Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WakeVox ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application WakeVox (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal data.

All data created within the App (alarms, timers, settings, and AI-generated voice prompts) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to our servers.

2. Voice Generation Feature

When you use the voice generation feature, the text prompt you enter is sent to a third-party AI service solely for the purpose of generating audio. We do not store, log, or share your prompts. Please refer to the third-party provider's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases processed entirely through Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information. All transactions are handled by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. Google may collect certain data as part of their services. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications / Alarms – to ring alarms and send reminders
  • Storage – to save generated audio files locally on your device
  • Internet – only for AI voice generation and in-app purchases

No permission is used to collect personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
