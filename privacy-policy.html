Privacy Policy Last updated: June 15, 2025 This Privacy Policy describes how Pleading Alarm ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application Clock – Pleading Alarm (the "App"). 1. Information We Collect We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal data. All data created within the App (alarms, timers, settings, and AI-generated voice prompts) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to our servers. 2. In-App Purchases The App offers in-app purchases processed entirely through Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information. All transactions are handled by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. 4. Third-Party Services The App uses Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. Permissions The App may request the following device permissions: Notifications / Alarms – to ring alarms and send reminders Storage – to save generated audio files locally on your device Internet – only for AI voice generation and in-app purchases No permission is used to collect personal data. 6.
